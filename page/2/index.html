对从前的青春爱情的回忆

Posted on 2023-03-28 Edited on 2023-04-04 In Chat , Ramblings

Memories of my youth

Introduction

This is the most missed, painful, and failed youth I know, and it is my own time. Will the love of young people feel more and more childish as they get older? Or, if you still don’t feel childish after a few years or more than ten years, it’s just because you haven’t grown up for so many years?

I have doubted myself and raised all kinds of questions of the above type to myself. But in the end, I found that what scares me the most is not the childishness of my personality, but my memory of that time is gradually disappearing, and it seems like two passers-by with the past. Still drifting away.

原文

这是我所知的最令人怀念、痛苦、也是最失败的青春，它是属于我自己的一段时光。年轻人的爱恋是否会随着年龄的增长而愈发被自己觉得幼稚？或者说，如果几年、十几年之后你仍不觉幼稚的话，只是因为你这么多年都没有成长？
我怀疑过自己，也对自己提出过如上类型的种种疑问。但终究我发现，最令我害怕的不是人格的幼稚，而是我对那段时光的记忆渐渐消失，似乎和过去像是两个擦肩而过的行人，就算我极力驻足原地，它也依旧渐行渐远。
